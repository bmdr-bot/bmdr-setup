## Approval Workflow

1. **Draft**: Policy owner creates initial draft
2. **Review**: Legal and Security review
3. **Approval**: CISO and CTO approve
4. **Board**: Major policies require Board approval
5. **Communication**: All staff notified and trained
6. **Acknowledgment**: Staff acknowledge understanding
7. **Enforcement**: Automated and manual enforcement
